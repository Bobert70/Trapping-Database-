Dead Animals

Dead animals, unlike caster, are a more traditional type of bait. This bait works by smelling so good to the local scavengers and carnivores that they just have to check it out. This bait is primarily used in trapping scavengers and omnivores, such as foxes, raccoon, and coyote. This bait is also effective against mink and bobcats, but the meat has to be fresh in order to be effective.
