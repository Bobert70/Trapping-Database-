Caster

Caster is an essential type of bait for beaver trapping. Unlike traditional baits that attract animals from how delicious they look or how good they smell, caster attracts beaver in another way. Beaver are extremely territorial, and they make mounds out of mud and caster to mark their territory. By making an artificial caster mound, we make the local beaver want to replace the mound with their own. Use caster by putting a mound where there is sign, and then putting a trap if front of the only access point to the caster. Do it so that the beaber has to go through the trap to get to the mound. You can get caster ny taking the glands from a dead beaver and smashing them up (sometimes you mix it with other stuff), or you can get it off Amazon.
